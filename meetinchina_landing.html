FAQ

Before you ask

Who exactly will meet me?

A MEET Certified interpreter: selected, trained at MEET Academy, examined, and tested on a supervised order. You see the profile — photo, languages, industries, certificate number — before you fly. Certificates are verifiable on this site.

Which cities do you cover?

Any major manufacturing or trade city: Guangzhou, Yiwu, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Hangzhou, Ningbo, Qingdao, Suzhou and beyond. If your factory is two hours from the nearest airport — that is a normal order, not an exception.

What does a remote order look like?

You send a brief: what to find, what to ask, what to photograph. Our interpreter walks the fair or the factory and you receive a private report page within 48 hours — photos, video, booth-by-booth contacts and prices, and observations from the floor.

What if the interpreter cancels?

A certified backup steps in — that is part of the order, not a favour. Your meeting happens.

Can you also check the supplier I find?

Yes. That is the next step on the ladder: a full verification — plant audit, documents, samples to an EU lab — and contracting through an EU entity. Ask for it from your report room or in the form above.
